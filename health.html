Health Risk Across the Workforce

Who was most at risk of contracting COVID?:

Based on a similar analysis conducted by Politico, we look specifically at the health risks workers faced in NYC based on the physical proximity (x-axis) and median occupational wage (y-axis), with the bubbles sized by total people employed in that job. (Hover to see)

Physical proximity - defined as distance to others as defined by O*NET
Median Occupational Wage - median wage for NYC located occupation. Data from ACS

The most dangerous occupation, with the highest physical proximity score (99) are dental assistants, who make $35,392 and employ 105,702 workers. The least affected jobs: Lawyers, Personal Financial Advisors, and General and Operations Managers, scoring between 34-55 on physical proximity and making between $161K-$210K.
