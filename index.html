The Impacts of a Pandemic on NYC's Workforce

About this Project

This website was created by two MIT graduate students for the Big Data, Visualization and Society (11.454) course final project. The project aims to discuss the impacts of COVID on New York City by answering the following three questions:

1. Where are the hard hit areas in NYC? And who are the workers most impacted by the pandemic?
2. Is there a pattern to the vulnerability of different workers to the pandemic, based on their income level? Type of jobs? Place of living?
3. What did the NYC workforce look like before covid? How has it changed over time? What were the most affected industries in NY State?
